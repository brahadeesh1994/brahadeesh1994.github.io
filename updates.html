  • June 2026

    My Ph.D. students presented contributed talks at the 22nd edition of the Annual Conference of the Academy of Discrete Mathematics and Applications & International Conference on Discrete Mathematics (IIT Madras), on 7 June 2026. Yash Chawda presented our joint work with Saraswati Girish Nanoti (IISc Bangalore), On the Majority Game Chromatic Number of Trees, and Vishnu spoke on Tight Bounds on the Carathéodory and Exchange Numbers in \(\triangle\)-Convexity.

    I am delighted to add that both talks were shortlisted for the Prof. S. S. Shrikhande Memorial Endowment and Prof. R. Balakrishnan Endowment Best Paper Awards, and Yash Chawda won the Prof. S. S. Shrikhande Memorial Endowment Best Paper Award.
