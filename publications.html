N. Balachandran and Brahadeesh Sankarnarayanan 5-list coloring toroidal 6-regular triangulations in linear time
2026
DAM 386, 75–103
DOI arXiv
Let $G$ be a $6$-regular triangulation of the torus. We consider the problem of $5$-list coloring for such graphs. We provide a linear time algorithm to achieve this coloring by exploiting the structural properties of the embedding and the Euler characteristic $\chi(S)$.
