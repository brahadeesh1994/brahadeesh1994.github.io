Research Focus

My work centers on Extremal Graph Theory and Combinatorics. Recent problems include:

  • Brualdi–Hoffman Problems: Investigating the maximum spectral radius of $F$-free graphs on $m$ edges, specifically where $F = K_4$.
  • Intersection Graphs: Representing graphs as vertex-intersection graphs of paths.
  • Graph Coloring: Studying the gap between chromatic number $\chi(G)$ and list chromatic number $\chi_l(G)$ for surface-embedded graphs.
